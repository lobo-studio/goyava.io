How works?

“Goyava is a proximity commerce and loyalty program application. It empowers users to find shops within their neighbourhoods to stimulate business within their communities and these in return reward users by providing points that can be traded into rewards. Keep your clients engaged, no IT setup, your clients never lose their points, get real-time statistics, create promotes and send them to your clients in a few clicks.”

Bolo Michelin. Goyava Inc.

Step 1

Buy a coffee

Why stop at coffee? when you can collect rewards from everything you purchase in neighborhood. Goyava works with any type of industries or products

Step 2

Take a shoot QR

You create an account in a few simple steps, create your virtual card and rewards. Each reward generates a unique QR Code. When your customers purchase an item qualified for rewards, you scan the QR code(s) and points are safely collected. The system is independent of your P.O.S. so there is not complex set up and the scan take less than a second.

Step 3

Collect your stamp!

The more you shop with goyava the ore rewards you will collect and the more you will contribute the the wealth of your neighbourghood.
